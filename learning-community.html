01 / My journey

Why this space exists.

My own path through technology has not always felt straightforward.

After 3.5 years of production frontend engineering at Jio Platforms, I began deliberately rebuilding and broadening my foundations. That process brought familiar difficulties: fundamentals I needed to revisit, too many course and technology choices, and AI tools that could produce answers before I had truly understood the problem.

I’m currently working through Python, data structures and algorithms, full-stack development, system design and applied AI. I am not presenting myself as someone who has mastered every part of that journey.

What I can offer beginners is a practical conversation from someone who has shipped production software and is still actively learning: helping you reduce confusion, assess your immediate situation and choose a manageable next step.
