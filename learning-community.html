01 / My journey

Why this space exists.

My own path through technology has not always felt straightforward.

I spent 3.5 years working on production frontend applications at Jio Platforms. After my role ended in January 2026, I began taking on independent requirements while deliberately rebuilding and broadening my foundations in full-stack engineering, system design and applied AI.

That process has brought familiar difficulties: revisiting forgotten fundamentals, choosing between too many courses and learning paths, following tutorials without always being able to apply the concepts, and using AI without losing independent problem-solving ability.

I am not presenting myself as someone who has mastered every part of that journey. I’m continuing to learn while helping other beginners get unstuck through practical, grounded conversations.
